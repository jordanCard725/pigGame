THE PIG GAME

- RULES -

  • The game has 2 players, playing in rounds
  • In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as he whishes. Each result gets added to his ROUND score
  • The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his ROUND score gets added to his TOTAL score. After you hold, it's the next player's turn
  • BUT, if the player rolls a 1, all of his ROUND score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn
  • If a player rolls two 6's in a row, all of his TOTAL score is lost. Then it's the next player's turn
  • The first player to reach a TOTAL score of the predetermiend 'Winning Score', wins the game
